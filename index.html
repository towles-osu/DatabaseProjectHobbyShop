Navigation hub for The Portland Hobby Shop delivery database

View Customer Database
This is a page where you can add, delete, or update new customers into the customer tables. Elements needed are the customers name, the customers phone number, and their address.
View Address Zone Information
The zones page is used to view delivery zones.
View Items In Stock
A database of all the items currently in inventory. Can add/delete items or manually update their current quantity.
View Items Sold Info
A database of all the Sold Items. Can be used to add/edit/delete records of items being sold.
