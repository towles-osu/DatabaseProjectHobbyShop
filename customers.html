Customer Display

SPECIAL NOTE The delete functions differently in this table based on whether the row displays a customer-address relationship or just a customer/just an address. Deleting a row with customer and address will remove the relationship between those two entities. Deleting a row with just a customer or just an address will delete that customer or address from the table. You must delete a customer or address from all its relationships before you can delete the customer or address itself.
