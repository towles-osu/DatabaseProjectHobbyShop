Add Customer and Address

Customers must have an address to be added, and vice versa. Use this field to add a new customer with an address that is not already in our system.

Add Just Customer

Customers must have an address to be added, and vice versa. Use this field to add a new customer with their address set to one already in our system.

Add Just Address

Addresses must have a Customer to be added, and vice versa. Use this field to add a new address for a customer that is already in our system.

Customer Display

SPECIAL NOTE The delete functions differently in this table based on whether the row displays a customer-address relationship or just a customer/just an address. Deleting a row with customer and address will remove the relationship between those two entities. Deleting a row with just a customer or just an address will delete that customer or address from the table. You must delete a customer or address from all its relationships before you can delete the customer or address itself.
